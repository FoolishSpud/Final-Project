What is Hollow Knight/when was it released?

Hollow Knight is a metroidvania that came out on February 24, 2017. The trailer can be viewed here.

Who made Hollow Knight?

It was created by Team Cherry, an Australia-based game studio who, at the time, consisted of 3 members: Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and Jack Vine. Their website can be found here.
