But what are some elements of the game that actually make the game good?

There are a TON of amazing things that go into making Hollow Knight, but just to name a few:

  1. Fluid, rebindable controls
  2. Intricately designed lore
  3. Intriguing characters
  4. Beautiful artwork and visually appealing design
  5. Amazing combat system
  6. Epic boss fights
  7. Difficult but rewarding platforming challenges
  8. A customizable playstyle through the game's charms
  9. A unique, wide, expansive, interconnected map that makes you earn all the pieces for it
  10. Great buildup to any battle or confrontation

But don't just take my word for it; other people feel the same way about the game.
